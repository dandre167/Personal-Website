The story behind the artist

My name is Andrea Caci. I’ve been creating art for as long as I can remember. Having that one thing you love and that you’re undeniably passionate about makes creating come as second nature. Finding pottery came to me on a whim and ever since it’s been my escape from reality. Pottery helped me find the fire within me to ignite such creativity that I didn’t even know was possible. You see to me art isn’t just something you create, it’s something that makes you want to live, something that makes you want to create, something that you know when people look at they will also fall in love with it the way you do. Being able to share my talent and create something that has meaning behind is so important to me. Thus Atlas by Andrea was born! .
